What is an interest area?

: a group of related academic programs that assists students in mapping a structured path to reach their educational & career goals, as well as facilitating them to network with peers who have a like-minded mindset that shared similar interests to theirs

The 6 Interest Areas (RIASEC)

1. Realistic: enjoy practical, hands-on work, such as designing/building something, repairing equipment, physical activity, or working outdoors

2. Investigate: searching for facts and solving problems; enjoy work that involves studying or researching, such as in a lab

3. Artistic: expressing through creative, original work; enjoy making visual art, performing, writing or designing something

4. Social: working with others to be learned and growed; enjoy teaching, giving advice, or physically caring for others

5. Enterprising: starting and managing business projects; enjoy leading or advising people, selling or marketing products, and conducting negotiations

6. Conventional: enjoy work that involves organizing information, often in an office setting; like projects that follow set procedures and regulations
